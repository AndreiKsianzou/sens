News
Enables you to create, channel and track reactions to the news
  • Inform your employees about what's most important and what's happening
  • Divide the news into categories and communicate it to selected departments
  • Let your employees react/comment on the news
  • View the reaction and statistics according to the specified criteria
KPI
Informs the user about his KPI structure and status
  • Increase motivation by letting social rating contribute to employee’s KPI
  • Browse through historical KPI performance
  • Enable your employees to dynamically track their KPI, along with KPI of their department/branch
Training and education
Helps you train and educate your employees
  • Upload/create training materials (we help you make it interactive)
  • Test the knowledge through the tests
  • Motivate employees using in app bonus points that affects KPI
  • Train newcomers faster and more efficiently using onboarding courses with checklists
Personal profile
Provides contact information and basic statistics about the user
  • Assign the user to the branch/department/function
  • Limit or grant access to required information streams
  • Display user’s social rating, formed by his engagement (number of news read, tests solved, etc)
Control panel
Provides a powerful tool to interact with employees’ reaction, feedback and performance
  • Generate, analyze business units’ activity and statistics of user groups
  • View and analyze the results of the questionnaires
  • View the engagement rating by department/group
  • Analyze the content (news) in terms of their popularity among users
  • Make more informed decisions using insights into employees’ reactions and usage
